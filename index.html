The Gift of Salvation

God loves you deeply and wants you to experience peace and eternal life through Jesus Christ. The Bible tells us that "all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God" (Romans 3:23), but the good news is that "God demonstrates His own love toward us, in that while we were still sinners, Christ died for us" (Romans 5:8).

Salvation is a free gift from God that we receive by faith. It's not about being good enough or doing enough good works - it's about accepting what Jesus has already done for you on the cross. "For by grace you have been saved through faith, and that not of yourselves; it is the gift of God, not of works, lest anyone should boast" (Ephesians 2:8-9).

Today, you can receive this gift by turning from your sins (repentance) and trusting in Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior. This decision will transform your life now and for eternity!

Salvation Prayer

"Heavenly Father, I come to You admitting that I am a sinner. I believe that Jesus Christ is Your Son, that He died on the cross for my sins, and that You raised Him from the dead. I turn from my sins and invite Jesus to come into my heart and life as my personal Lord and Savior. I want to trust and follow Him as my Lord from this day forward. Thank You, Lord, for saving me and forgiving me! In Jesus' name I pray, Amen."

If you prayed this prayer, we'd love to celebrate with you! Contact us and let us know about your decision to follow Christ.

About The Cross Garment

The Cross Garment was born from a passion to share the Gospel through fashion. Our mission is simple: to create high-quality, faith-inspired clothing that sparks conversations about Jesus Christ.

Each design is carefully crafted to not just look good, but to carry a message of hope, love, and salvation. When you wear our shirts, you're not just making a fashion statement - you're becoming a walking testimony of God's love.

We believe that something as simple as a t-shirt can be used by God to open doors for spiritual conversations. A portion of every sale goes to support missions and evangelism efforts around the world.
